Connect your X (formerly Twitter) account to share your content and track engagement metrics.

## Prerequisites

- An X account in good standing
- Account email verified
- Two-factor authentication recommended

## Account Requirements

- Standard X accounts supported
- Account must not be restricted
- Active email verification
- You can connect multiple X accounts based on your subscription plan

## Connecting Your Account

1. Go to [BeeHype Integrations](https://www.beehype.io/app/integrations)
2. In the X section, click "Connect"
3. Log in to X if prompted
4. Grant required permissions:
   - Read tweet access
   - Basic posting access
   - User profile information

## Required Permissions

BeeHype needs these permissions to:

- **Tweet Reading**
  - Access your tweets
  - View engagement metrics
  - Monitor performance

- **Tweet Writing**
  - Post new content
  - Schedule tweets
  - Manage media uploads

- **Profile Access**
  - Read account info
  - View followers/following
  - Access analytics

- **Offline Access**
  - Schedule future posts
  - Maintain connection
  - Track analytics

## Content Guidelines

X has specific requirements for posts:

1. **Text Posts**
   - Maximum length: 280 characters
   - Supports mentions and hashtags
   - URLs count as 23 characters

2. **Images**
   - Up to 4 images per tweet
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
   - Maximum file size: 5MB per image

3. **Videos**
   - Maximum length: 2 minutes 20 seconds
   - Maximum file size: 512MB
   - Supported formats: MP4

## Managing Your Connection

### Disconnecting Account
1. Visit the Integrations page
2. Click "Remove" next to X
3. Confirm disconnection

<Warning>
Disconnecting will cancel all scheduled posts and stop analytics tracking. You can reconnect at any time.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:

1. **Connection Issues**
   - Verify your email is confirmed on X
   - Make sure your account is in good standing
   - Try disconnecting and reconnecting your account

2. **Posts Not Publishing**
   - Check if your content follows X guidelines
   - Ensure your account is active
   - Try disconnecting and reconnecting your account

3. **Analytics Not Showing**
   - Give it some time, data can take a while to appear
   - Check if your account is still connected
   - Try refreshing your connection by disconnecting and reconnecting

If problems persist, contact [support@beehype.io](mailto:support@beehype.io)
