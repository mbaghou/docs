Connect your TikTok Creator account to automatically publish videos and track performance metrics.

## Prerequisites

- A TikTok Creator account
- Account in good standing
- Age requirement met (16+ or 18+ depending on region)

## Account Requirements

### Creator Account
- Must be a Creator account (not personal)
- Business accounts not supported
- Account must be public
- You can connect multiple Creator accounts based on your subscription plan

### Converting to Creator Account
1. Go to TikTok Settings
2. Tap "Manage Account"
3. Switch to "Creator Account"
4. Complete profile setup

## Connecting Your Account

1. Go to [BeeHype Integrations](https://www.beehype.io/app/integrations)
2. In the TikTok section, click "Connect"
3. Log in to TikTok if prompted
4. Grant required permissions:
   - Basic user information
   - Profile access
   - Video upload rights
   - Analytics access

## Content Guidelines

TikTok has specific requirements for video content:

1. **Videos**
   - Length: 15 seconds to 10 minutes
   - Format: MP4, MOV
   - Resolution: 1080x1920 recommended
   - Maximum file size: 500MB

2. **Captions**
   - Maximum length: 2,200 characters
   - Hashtag limit: Recommended 3-5
   - Mention limit: 10 per post

3. **Sound**
   - Original audio supported
   - Music must be licensed
   - Clear audio quality required

## Managing Your Connection

### Disconnecting Account
1. Visit the Integrations page
2. Click "Remove" next to TikTok
3. Confirm disconnection

<Warning>
Disconnecting will cancel all scheduled posts and stop analytics tracking. You can reconnect at any time.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:

1. **Connection Problems**
   - Make sure you have a Creator account
   - Verify you meet the age requirements
   - Try disconnecting and reconnecting your account

2. **Videos Not Publishing**
   - Check if your videos follow TikTok guidelines
   - Ensure your account is active
   - Try disconnecting and reconnecting your account

3. **Analytics Not Available**
   - Give it some time, data can take a while to appear
   - Check if your account is still connected
   - Try refreshing your connection by disconnecting and reconnecting

If problems persist, contact [support@beehype.io](mailto:support@beehype.io)
