Connect your LinkedIn profile to automatically share your content with your professional network and track engagement.

## Prerequisites

- A LinkedIn personal profile
- Profile must be in good standing
- Email address verified on LinkedIn

## Important Notes

- Only personal profiles are supported (not company pages)
- You can connect multiple LinkedIn profiles based on your subscription plan
- Publishing permissions required for automated posting

## Connecting Your Account

1. Go to [BeeHype Integrations](https://www.beehype.io/app/integrations)
2. In the LinkedIn section, click "Connect"
3. Log in to LinkedIn if prompted
4. Grant required permissions:
   - OpenID for authentication
   - Profile access
   - Post publishing rights (w_member_social)
   - Email verification


## Required Permissions Explained

BeeHype requires specific permissions to:

- **OpenID**
  - Secure authentication
  - Account verification

- **Profile Access**
  - Read basic profile info
  - Access professional network
  - View post history

- **Publishing Rights (w_member_social)**
  - Create posts
  - Schedule content
  - Manage article sharing

- **Email Verification**
  - Account security
  - Communication preferences

## Content Guidelines

LinkedIn has specific requirements for professional content:

1. **Text Posts**
   - Maximum length: 3,000 characters
   - Supports line breaks and formatting
   - Hashtag limit: Recommended 3-5

2. **Images**
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG
   - Optimal size: 1200x627 pixels
   - Maximum file size: 5MB

3. **Articles**
   - Title length: Up to 100 characters
   - Content length: Up to 110,000 characters
   - Can include multiple images

## Managing Your Connection

### Disconnecting Account
1. Visit the Integrations page
2. Click "Remove" next to LinkedIn
3. Confirm disconnection

<Warning>
Disconnecting will cancel all scheduled posts and stop analytics tracking. You can reconnect at any time.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:

1. **Connection Problems**
   - Make sure your email is verified on LinkedIn
   - Check if your profile is in good standing
   - Try disconnecting and reconnecting your account

2. **Posts Not Publishing**
   - Verify your content follows LinkedIn guidelines
   - Ensure your account is active
   - Try disconnecting and reconnecting your account

3. **Analytics Not Available**
   - Give it some time, data can take a while to appear
   - Check if your account is still connected
   - Try refreshing your connection by disconnecting and reconnecting

If problems persist, contact [support@beehype.io](mailto:support@beehype.io)
